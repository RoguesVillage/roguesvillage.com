Rogues Village

Village Description

Rogues Village is a place to explore alternative approaches to and uses for existing security concepts by looking to non-traditional areas of knowledge. Incorporating expertise from the worlds of sleight of hand, con games, espionage, and advantage play, this village has a special emphasis on Social Engineering, Physical Security and Playful Mischief.

FAQ:

"What is Defcon?"

DEFCON is one of the largest and longest running hacking conferences in the world, taking place annually in Las Vegas. More than 25,000 participants attended last year to listen to cutting edge talks and experience newly released tools and exploits.

"What is a village?"

A major part of DEFCON are its 'villages,' individual rooms at the conference dedicated to specific topics such as IoT, WiFi security, lock picking, bio-hacking, and many, many others.

"What is Four Suits Co.?"

Four Suits Co. is a creative studio focused on magic as a tool in theater, security, and design. Our team consists of sleight-of-hand and mentalism performers, OSCP specialists, visual artists, and product designers. While our skills and backgrounds are diverse, we have a shared mission: to introduce fresh, engaging, and thoughtful experiences of magic to the world.
